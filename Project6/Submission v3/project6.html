Person-To-Person Lending

Prosper Loans offers loan financed by crowd sourcing funds from ordinary people looking for investments. This is known as person-to-person lending. Prosper records for these loans if a friend has contributed financing. These charts look for variation in the number and proportion of 2008 Prosper loan turnout, whether the loan was paid or defaulted, between those loans that had contributions from friends and those that did not.
